Help me design a new sass application hosted on next.js, called fivestarz. This application is for any online entrepreneur who wants more reviews. While, most review platforms focus on pestering your customers until they finally relent and leave a review. This application is completely different.
This application allows members like a club to review each other’s stuff, ethically, legally and profitably. The way it works as we match up two people to provide feedback to each other’s service or thing they each leave personal feedback for the other person within our app and then they can decide whether or not to push that to a real review online to the platform of their choice. Each party is not obligated to leave a positive review. The person who receives the feedback cannot post that as a review online - they can ask the person who provided the feedback to do it and that other person can choose from the Channels shown by the person who is asking. We need to closely track who left reviews for who to ever allow those same two people to review each other again. In addition, Paid users can set their degrees of Personal separation from 1 to 3. If 1 then they will never review the sane Person OR any one who reviewed or was reviewed BY that sane person. Make sense? The default setting is 1.
Our app provides a simple form for uploading screenshots, descriptions and links to assets each person wants Reviewed. Paid users can build multiple assets to collect reviews on all of them at the same time (within plan limits), each asset must have a unique URL and unique review profile listings.
Any asset in our system can have multiple review CHANNELS to allow the other person to choose a channel to leave review on, or they can have just 1 channel - typically Google Business Profile, Yelp profile, Trip Advisor Listing Amazon, or Shopify page.
Feedback can be be of anything including: One person gives the other a free 30 minute business growth consultation, so the person getting the free consult can provide Feedback to the consultant and the consultant can provide feedback to user about their overall impressions about that persons business.
Paid members are also allowed to gather Reviews for their client’s. If it makes sense ethically - example would be they have a Plumber as a client and that Plumber posts a video to their Google Business Profile about “how to avoid clogged sinks” - the other person watches the video and can leave Feedback about it and if they leaned anything. Then the paid user can decide whether to ask that other person to post the feedback as a real review to the client’s review channel of choice.
Free members get 4 matches / month. Paid members get 12.
Our application has a how-to page with steps and rules to providing feedback to others and the things that will get you kicked out of the club: Leaving positive feedback in all then negative reviews online, being mean or disrespectful to other members, trying to game the system by creating multiple diff accounts. We want our platform to be friendly, welcoming and productive with happy users.
Theme should be warm and friendly like mailchimp. Our target market is solopreneuers and founders trying to build up their online social proof. Ask any questions
